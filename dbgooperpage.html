D.B. Gooper

D.B. Gooper was one of the first games that I worked on. Play as D.B. Gooper as you fall from a plane, collecting money on the way down. avoid going splat on various falling obstacles, and acquire power-ups to help you as you fall.

I served as the primary gameplay programmer and designer: I implemented player movement, obstacle design & spawning, power-up design & implementation, and I created many art assets.
