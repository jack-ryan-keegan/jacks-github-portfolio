Minecraft Modding

I developed a few Minecraft mods for Minecraft versions 1.12.1 and 1.16.2. These mods contained new blocks, items, biomes, and interactions

I designed all of the new blocks and items, ensuring that they would try to emulate some of vanilla Minecraft's design philosophies. I implemented these new features in Java, and textured each new addition myself. Some features were silver lanterns that repelled mobs, branching armor tiers that needed to be mined from the bottom of the End islands, plated tin barrels that were immune to explosions, and much more.
