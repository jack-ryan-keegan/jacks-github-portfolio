Pull It Together

Haul your wagon and survive through the wilderness with up to three friends in this casual, cooperative, multiplayer experience!

Pull It Together is a first person, 3D, co-op, survival/adventure game, with the goal of pulling your wagon to the end of a procedurally generated stretch of wilderness. As principal game designer, it was my responsibility to develop, ideate on,balance, and implement many of our mechanics: food, items, & supplies, the wagon, player health energy, trinkets and their effects, heaving, and more. I was also head of our external art team, and I requested, gave feedback on, and implemented much of the assets throughout the game.
