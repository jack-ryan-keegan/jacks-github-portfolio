Pull It Together

Haul your wagon and survive through the wilderness with up to three friends in this casual, cooperative, multiplayer experience!

Pull It Together is a first person, 3D, co-op, survival/adventure game, with the goal of pulling your wagon to the end of a procedurally generated stretch of wilderness. I served as the principal game designer on the team, and I am in charge of designing and implementing items, trinkets, foods, and what each of them do. In addition, I implemented and balanced the wagon, balanced the storm and player health & energy, implemented art & ui assets

Bait & Tackle has been published, and is available for free on Steam here: Bait & Tackle Steam Page
